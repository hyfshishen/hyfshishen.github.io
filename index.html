Yafan Huang (黄亚凡)

 

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Iowa. I am a member of IOWA-HPC Group and advised by Dr. Guanpeng Li. I am also a research intern in Argonne National Laboratory and supervised by Dr. Sheng Di. Before coming to UIowa, I received my Master's degree (Sep.2018-Jun.2021, major: Computer Science) at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan. I was a member of Cluster and Grid Computing Lab (CGCL) lead by Dr. Hai Jin and advised by Dr. Feng Zhao. I received my Bachelor's degree (Sep.2014-Jun.2018, major: Software Engineering) at Hunan University, Changsha. I spent a happy time there :)
