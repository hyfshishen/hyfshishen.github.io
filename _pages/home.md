We are a dynamic research group at the [Computer Science Department of UIowa](https://cs.uiowa.edu/). Our aim is to explore and understand High Performance Computing (HPC), including acceleration, resilience, and system dependability.

We are located at [University of Iowa](https://uiowa.edu/), a public research university in Iowa City, Iowa. On an urban 1,880-acre campus on the banks of the Iowa River, the University of Iowa is classified among "R1: Doctoral Universities – Very high research activity". The HPC in UIowa ranked 38th in United States based on [CSRankings](http://csrankings.org/#/index?hpc&us).

 **We are always looking for passionate and self-motivated new PhD students, Postdocs, and Master students to join the team** [(more info)]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/vacancies) **!**
